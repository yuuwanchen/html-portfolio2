The Best Movies According to Teresa

My Top 3 Movies

Little Women

The story is mainly talking about the life of four sisters in the Civil War era. They are very close to each other and always support each other. The story is very touching and inspiring. I like the character Jo the most, she is very independent and brave. She is also very talented in writing. The movie is very well made, the costumes and sets are very beautiful. The acting is also very good, especially Saoirse Ronan who plays Jo. I highly recommend this movie to everyone.

The Green Book

The story is based on a true story, it is about a black pianist and his white driver in the 1960s. The movie shows the racism and discrimination

Inception

The story is about a thief who can enter people's dreams and steal their secrets. He is hired to plant an idea in someone's mind. The movie is very exciting and full of twists and turns. The special effects are amazing, especially the scenes in the dream world. The acting is also very good, especially Leonardo DiCaprio who plays the main character. I highly recommend this movie to everyone.s
